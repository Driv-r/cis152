About Us

How We Began

Over 15 years ago, Pacific Trails Resort started off as an itty bitty hotel located on the coast west of Los Angeles called the Pacific Hotel. We specialized in making a wonderful breakfast for our loyal customers. The Pacific Hotel was often had all of our rooms full. Our guests noted how wonderful the scenery was at the hotel, and they started asking if there were any hiking trails they could go out on. It was at this point that we realized that our hotel was more than just a hotel. We used our savings to path various hiking trails, purchase kayaks for guest use, and build yurts so you could be as close to nature as possible, ultimately turning in the Pacific Trails Resort so many of our guests love today.

Why Us?

Here at Pacific Trails Resort, we have always prioritized our guests before anything else. want to make sure that you feel welcome and appreciated. All of our employees have a bleeding heart for nature. You'll be sure to find inner peace with your surroundings, whether it be through a face-to-face experience on one of our many hiking trails, admiring the beautiful sunrise and sunset on the ocean in one of our state of the art kayaks, or just taking it easy in the comfort of one of our hand-crafted yurts. However you choose to enjoy Mother Nature, we hope you choose us and we hope you enjoy your stay.
